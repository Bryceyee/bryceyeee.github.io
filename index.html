Projects

This page is dedicated to my favorite projects. I 3D printed a model of my design. I wanted to create furniture with biomimicry in mind. Here I created containers, shelves, or drawers that can move like a snake. It's mobility helps the owner build it around their other furniture istead of the other way around.

The two images on the right are two of my favorite photos that i've taken. Photography is somethinng that I frequently practice. Outside of designing or creating something new, I truly enjoy photography and capturing moments that tell stories. The image on the left is a landscape shot that I took of a small abandoned structure. The one next to it is an artistic piece. I made a collage and ripped it into pieces ad photographed it.

Here are some of my animations. Digital art is one of my favorite things to do. I really enjoy the process and all the trials and errors. It makes the learning process fun and challenging. The work with words that are constantly moving is my favorite. I really enjoy how simple yet aesthetic and poetic it is.
